The Concept

Beyond Footwear is an exhibition born from a question: what if shoes did more than protect your feet or complete an outfit? What if they responded to the environment, supported the body, and were made with the planet in mind?

Each of the three designs in this collection takes a distinct approach — one centered on nature's rhythms, one on personal safety, one on closed-loop sustainability — but all share a commitment to purposeful design.

This project challenges the conventions of footwear design and asks us to imagine a future where what we wear on our feet reflects our values.

The Collection

Design 01

The Four Seasons

Dig in without the ache.

A gardening shoe engineered to reduce gardening-related injuries through a highly innovative multi-surface, puncture-proof sole. Lightweight materials and phase-changing material technology keep it comfortable all day. Its lug-patterned sole features varied shapes and channels designed for traction across surfaces and weather conditions.

Puncture-proof Multi-surface Lightweight Injury prevention

Design 02

Breakaway

Safe. Secure. Supported.

Rethinking women's footwear to interconnect personal safety with bunion support. Breakaway gives women a hands-free way to illuminate their walks and an accessible emergency panic button that sends their location to the police. Comfort and security, every step of the way.

Women's safety Bunion support Emergency alert Hands-free

Design 03

BioStride

Performs on the street. Belongs to the earth.

A 100% biodegradable shoe engineered from upcycled avocado peels and natural cork. Synthetic adhesives are eliminated entirely — assembly relies on natural jute, hemp fibers, and latex as a biological binder. Avocado skins provide a durable, flexible base rich in antimicrobial phenols; cork offers water resistance, shock absorption, and hypoallergenic comfort.

Biodegradable Upcycled Zero synthetics Closed-loop
